# Code of Conduct

This Code of Conduct applies org-wide to all **Vocaler AI** repositories, spaces, and community interactions that do not define their own.

We adopt the **[Contributor Covenant, v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)**.

## Our pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards
Examples of positive behavior: empathy and kindness, respect for differing opinions, graceful acceptance of constructive feedback, and focusing on what is best for the community. Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, publishing others' private information, and other conduct reasonably considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement
Report unacceptable behavior to **hi@vocaler.ai**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

Full text and enforcement guidelines: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
